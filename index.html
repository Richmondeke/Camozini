About Our Chamber

The Mozambique-Nigeria Chamber of Commerce, a non profit non political organization, registered (Reg. nr. 100753545) and gazetted (boletim da Republica) under  Mozambican laws, is envisioned as a bridge between the economies and peoples of two great African Nations, Mozambique and Nigeria.

About the Founder

Dr. Armah Elibe

Is a Nigerian from a lineage of academics and teachers, in a region with a predominant culture of trading and general commerce as ethnic identity.

This trailblazing scientist, teacher, visionary, and creative thinker had his primary, and secondary education in Nigeria.

A proud Alumnus of Nigeria’s foremost University of Nigeria Nsukka, (UNN) with other qualifications from Johannesburg RSA, and Cambridge UK.

Dr. Armah Elibe is a scientist turned marketer, business development, investment and management person with a passion for the promotion of intra-African trade and investment, deploying pan African trade/ investment initiatives for collaborative engagements and international networking.

He is an apostle of youth and women economic empowerment ( through Agriculture) as a tool for economic development in sub Saharan Africa.

In addition to the Mozambique-Nigeria Chamber of Commerce (camozini), he also founded the International Coalition for the Development of Tourism in Africa (INCODEVTA), as well as the Afro Food Festival, Mozambique 2024, a trade mark of which he sits as Managing Director.

He has 2 sons from a Mozambican mother.

He is a passionate pan Africanist who loves developing MSMEs.
